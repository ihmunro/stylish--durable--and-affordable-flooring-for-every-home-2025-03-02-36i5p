FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our flooring solutions and services.

How long does installation typically take?

Most residential installations take 1-3 days, depending on the square footage and flooring type. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your home or business. Larger commercial projects may take 5-7 days.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We provide complimentary in-home consultations where our flooring experts will bring samples, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

What types of flooring do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range including hardwood, engineered wood, luxury vinyl, laminate, tile, carpet, and specialty floors like cork and bamboo. Each comes with various style, color, and finish options.

What's included in your warranty?

All our installations come with a minimum 2-year workmanship warranty. Product warranties vary by manufacturer but typically range from 10-25 years for residential installations. We're happy to provide detailed warranty information for your specific project.

Do you handle floor preparation and removal?

Absolutely. Our comprehensive service includes removal and disposal of your existing flooring, subfloor preparation, and any necessary repairs before installation begins.
